Brief:

This class project was to find a solution to accommodate both students who are struggling and excelling in a classroom.

Our Solution:

How to accommodate both students who are struggling and excelling in a classroom.

This approach is perfectly summarised in our project slogan:

"Everybody has something to offer. Everybody has something to gain."

We figured not everyone is "good" at everything and may be lacking in other skills. Communication, Design, Certain Tech, Etc.

Our solution was to create an online platform, Skill Exchange, where students are able to take a comprehensive skills quiz to identify any knowledge gaps. Subsequently the result of the quiz pairs them with other students according to both of their learning needs.

More:

As we think everyone has something new to learn and students struggling or excelling will be learning new skills together, it would be a great solution to even out the field in the classroom.

The idea behind this application was to use IBM's Watson API to generate quizes for your current skill level, and then pair you up with another student(s) who may be more or less experienced in other fields. This will allow students to study with other students in the classroom and learn together.
