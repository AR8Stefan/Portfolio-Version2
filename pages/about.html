About Myself ~

I am a former student from Coder Academy in Sydney, Australia. I really enjoy front-end web development, and the challenges that come with it. When I'm not coding I like to watch movies, go for a walk, play video games and more.

My passion for creating things came when I was very young. I'd play with lego and clay into mould animals and dinosuars.

When I left high school I wanted to become a pastry chef. But I couldn't find a job, I then worked with my father in construction till I saved enough money to study something else I thought I'd enjoy. After I completed the I.T. Networking course I knew I didnt want to pursue it and went back to working with my father while also seeking a job to become that pastry chef I wanted to become.

At the end of July in 2016, I suffered from a slipped disc in my lower spine. My doctors told me not to continue working in construction as it would further damage my spine, then came the search for a new career.

I didn't know what I wanted to do after my injury besides "pushing papers". My now fiancée suggested Web-Development! I decided to look into it and try a few trials. It was like magic.
