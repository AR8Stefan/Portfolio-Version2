About Myself ~

I am a former student from Coder Academy in Sydney, Australia. I really enjoy front-end development, and the challenges that come with it. When I'm not coding I like to watch movies, go for a walk, play video games and more.

My passion for creating things came when I was very young. I played with lego and I loved moulding clay into animals and dinosuars.

When I left high school I wanted to become a pastry chef, but I couldn't find a job. I then worked with my father in construction till I saved enough money to study I.T. Networking which I thought would be fun.. It wasn't for me. After I completed the I.T. Networking course I knew I didnt want to pursue it and went back to working with my father while also seeking a job to still become a pastry chef.

At the end of July in 2016, I suffered from a slipped disc in my lower spine. My doctors told me not to continue working in construction or anything taxing on my body as it would further damage my spine. Thus the search for a new career began.

I didn't know what I wanted to do after my injury besides "pushing papers". My now fiancée suggested Web-Development! I decided to look into it and try a few trials. It was like magic. I studied on my own until I could enroll in Coder Academy in February 2017.

Since then I have graduated from Coder Academy and moved to Austin, USA. Coding and learning new things helped pass time while waiting for immigration and the prper paperwork to allow me to work. I can't wait to get out there and start making a differnce.

My Hobbies ~

My hobbies include: Watching Movies, PLaying Video Games, Listening to Music and Driving RC Cars.
